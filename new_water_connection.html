Steps to Apply for a New Water Connection

1. Application Form

Download or collect an application form from our offices or website.

2. Submit Documents

Submit the completed form with necessary identification and property documents.

3. Site Inspection

Our technical team will visit your premises to inspect and assess feasibility.

4. Payment

Pay the applicable connection fee as advised after inspection.

5. Installation

Our team will install the water connection at your property.

6. Activation

Once installed, your connection will be activated and ready to use.
