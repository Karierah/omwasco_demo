Steps to Request Exhaust Services

1. Submit Request

Visit our office or call customer service to request exhaust services.

2. Schedule Inspection

A field officer may be dispatched to inspect the site if necessary.

3. Pay Service Fee

Pay the applicable fee as guided by our billing team or notice board.

4. Exhaustion Scheduled

The team will schedule a date and time to dispatch the exhaust truck.

5. Completion

Once the service is complete, the customer receives a confirmation report.
