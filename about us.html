OTHAYA-MUKURWEINI WATER AND SANITATION PLC.

Who We Are

Othaya-Mukurweini has seen numerous reforms in the water sector since the Water Act 2002, which was founded on efficiency, sustainability, and affordability. OMWASCO was incorporated on May 29, 2006, under this act and began operations on July 7, 2006, as a water services provider. It now operates under Nyeri County, following the Water Act 2016 and Nyeri County Water Act 2016.

OMWASCO is incorporated under the Companies Act 2015 and is appointed by the Tana Water Services Board and Nyeri County Government. The company provides water and sewerage services in Nyeri South and Mukurweini sub-counties. It operates as a public company limited by guarantee, ensuring autonomy and commercial operation without share capital. Membership includes stakeholders and the public.

Our mandate includes maintaining water assets, implementing asset management practices, efficient resource use, and expanding water service accessibility. We aim to meet current demands and support sustainable infrastructure growth.
